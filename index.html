History Of Photography

The art and practice of creating images by recording light, has evolved from early chemical processes to the digital age we know today. The journey began with the camera obscura, a natural phenomenon used for centuries, and progressed through the invention of the daguerreotype and calotype processes, which paved the way for modern photography.
